ABOUT

Who we are

We are a small group of Software Development professsionals concerned about the Climate Emergency. We are not in any way eminent but we are competent.

We created this site and associated resouces because we wanted to do justice to what we think is a good idea. We have no financial or business motivation or goals. All of this was done in our spare time and for the "public good".

We would be delighted with any interest or feedback from anyone, positive or negative. If you have some killer reason why FLAP can't work please drop a quick line to tell us so we can get back to Twitter. If you have software development skills and are interested in helping with a full scale open source implementation we would be delighted to hear from you. There is a lot more that could be done.

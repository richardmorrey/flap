ABOUT

Who we are

I am a Software Development professsional concerned about the Climate Emergency. I am not in any way eminent but I am competent.

I created this site and associated resouces because I wanted to do justice to what I still think is a good idea. I have no financial or business motivation or goals. All of this was done in my spare time and for the "public good".

I would be delighted with any interest or feedback from anyone, positive or negative. If you have some killer reason why FLAP can't work please drop a quick line to tell us so we can get back to Twitter. If you have software development skills and are interested in helping with a full scale open source implementation I would be delighted to hear from you. There is a lot more that could be done.
